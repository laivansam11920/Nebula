Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically when you use our services, and information from third-party sources. The types collected depend on how you use Novacore and the permissions you grant.

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. You can review and delete your data at any time through account settings.

Category Examples Purpose
Account Data Name, email, password hash, profile photo Authentication, personalization
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, session duration Product improvement, analytics
Device Data IP address, browser type, OS, device ID Security, compatibility
Payment Data Last 4 digits, billing address (via Stripe) Transaction processing
Communications Support messages, email correspondence Customer support, legal compliance

How We Use Your Data

Section 02

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platform and services
  • Process transactions and send related communications
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services over time
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Sharing & Disclosure

Section 03

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties strictly to operate our services:

  • Service providers — Stripe, AWS, Datadog (under DPA)
  • Legal compliance — when required by law or subpoena
  • Business transfers — merger or acquisition only
  • With consent — any other sharing requires your explicit approval
Section 04

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Data Type Retention Period Deletion Method
Account information Duration of account + 30 days post-closure Secure wipe, cryptographic erasure
Usage & analytics logs 13 months (rolling) Automated purge pipeline
Billing records 7 years (regulatory requirement) Restricted access, eventual purge
Support tickets 3 years from resolution Anonymization then deletion
Backup snapshots 90 days Automated overwrite cycle
Section 05

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. We honor all requests within 30 days of receipt.

GDPR Rights (EU/EEA)

  • Right of access to your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restrict processing

CCPA Rights (California)

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sale (N/A)
  • Right to non-discrimination
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to limit use of sensitive data

Security Measures

Section 07

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

  • AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication enforcement
  • Annual third-party penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure (AWS)
  • 72-hour breach notification per GDPR Article 33
  • Dedicated security incident response team

International Transfers

Section 08

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions.

Primary data: AWS us-east-1 · EU replication: AWS eu-west-1

Children's Privacy

Section 09

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16 (or 13 in the United States). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover a child's data was collected without parental consent, we will delete it immediately. Contact privacy@novacore.io.
